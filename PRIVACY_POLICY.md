# Privacy Policy for SCI-Assist Bot

**Effective Date:** August 18, 2025  
**Last Updated:** August 18, 2025

## 1. Introduction

SCI-Assist ("we," "our," or "the bot") is a Discord bot designed to provide specialized assistance for the spinal cord injury (SCI) community. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with the highest standards of care and transparency.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with SCI-Assist on Discord.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Discord Messages**: Conversations you have with the bot
- **Commands**: Slash commands and interactions you use
- **Consent Preferences**: Your privacy settings and data storage consent

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically
- **Discord User ID**: Your unique Discord identifier (for conversation continuity)
- **Discord Username**: Your Discord username and display name
- **Timestamp Data**: When you interact with the bot
- **Server Information**: Which Discord server the interaction occurred in

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Voice Data**: We do not process or store voice communications
- **File Contents**: We do not access files you share in Discord
- **Private Messages**: We only respond to direct mentions or commands
- **Other Discord Activity**: We do not monitor your other Discord activities

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:
- **Consent**: You explicitly consent to data storage through our privacy controls
- **Legitimate Interest**: Providing SCI community support and improving our services
- **Contract Performance**: Delivering the bot services you request

## 4. How We Use Your Information

### 4.1 Primary Uses
- **Conversation Continuity**: Maintaining context across your interactions
- **Personalized Responses**: Providing relevant SCI-related assistance
- **Service Improvement**: Enhancing bot responses and capabilities

### 4.2 Secondary Uses (With Consent)
- **Community Training Data**: Anonymized conversations to improve responses for all users
- **Research Purposes**: Supporting SCI community research (fully anonymized)

## 5. Data Storage and Retention

### 5.1 Storage Location
- **Self-Hosted**: All data is stored on our own servers (not third-party clouds)
- **Secure Infrastructure**: Industry-standard security measures
- **Geographic Location**: [Specify your server location]

### 5.2 Retention Periods
- **Operational Data**: 7 days for immediate conversation context

- Daily automated processes remove expired data

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 Consent Control
- **Grant Consent**: Use `/privacy` command to enable data storage
- **Revoke Consent**: Withdraw consent at any time through `/privacy`
- **Granular Control**: Choose what types of data to store

### 6.2 Data Access Rights
- **View Data**: See all data we have about you through `/privacy`
- **Export Data**: Download all your data via `/data_export`
- **Correct Data**: Request corrections to your stored information

### 6.3 Data Deletion Rights
- **Delete All Data**: Use `/delete_data` for complete removal
- **Partial Deletion**: Remove specific conversations or data types
- **Right to be Forgotten**: Complete erasure from all systems

## 7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 7.1 We Do NOT Share Your Data With
- Third-party companies or services
- Advertisers or marketers
- Government agencies (except as legally required)
- Other Discord servers or bots

### 7.2 Limited Sharing Scenarios
- **Legal Compliance**: Only if required by valid legal process
- **Safety Concerns**: Serious threats to health or safety
- **Technical Support**: Anonymized data for troubleshooting only

## 8. Security Measures

### 8.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Strict authentication and authorization
- **Regular Updates**: Security patches and system updates
- **Monitoring**: Continuous security monitoring and logging

### 8.2 Operational Safeguards
- **Limited Access**: Only authorized personnel can access data
- **Audit Trails**: All data access is logged and monitored
- **Incident Response**: Procedures for handling security incidents

## 10. Children's Privacy

SCI-Assist is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
